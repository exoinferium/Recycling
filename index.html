Why Should We Recycle?

🌍

Protect Our Planet

Recycling reduces the need for new raw materials, preserving natural resources and reducing environmental damage from mining and logging.

⚡

Save Energy

Manufacturing products from recycled materials uses significantly less energy than creating them from scratch, reducing carbon emissions.

💰

Economic Benefits

Recycling creates jobs in collection, processing, and manufacturing, while reducing landfill costs for communities.

🏭

Reduce Pollution

Less landfill waste means reduced soil and water contamination, creating healthier environments for all living things.

How to Recycle Properly

1

Clean & Dry

Rinse containers and let them dry to prevent contamination

2

Sort Materials

Separate plastics, metals, glass, and paper according to your local guidelines

3

Remove Lids

Take off caps and lids from bottles and jars before recycling

4

Flatten Boxes

Flatten cardboard boxes to save space in recycling bins

5

Check Local Rules

Different communities have different recycling guidelines—know yours!

Recycling Guide

Cardboard
✅ RECYCLABLE - Flatten and break down before recycling
🍾
Glass Bottles
✅ RECYCLABLE - Rinse thoroughly, keep separate from trash

Aluminum Cans
✅ RECYCLABLE - Highest value recyclable, always accepted
📰
Newspapers
✅ RECYCLABLE - Keep dry and bundle together
🥤
Plastic Bags
❌ NOT RECYCLABLE - Tangled in machinery, take to store instead
🍕
Pizza Boxes
❌ NOT RECYCLABLE (if greasy) - Contaminated with food
📄
Paper
✅ RECYCLABLE - Most paper products are recyclable
🔋
Batteries
❌ NOT RECYCLABLE (in trash) - Take to special collection sites
